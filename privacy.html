Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

StyleTally ("StyleTally", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the StyleTally mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address.
  • Profile data: Style preferences, gender category, AI personality selection, and notification settings you provide within the app.
  • Usage data: How often you use features such as outfit analysis and colour analysis, to enforce free tier limits and improve the app.
  • Images: Photos you upload for outfit or colour analysis. These are processed to generate style feedback and are not stored.
  • Device data: Device type, operating system version, and push notification tokens for delivering daily reminders.
  • Purchase data: Subscription status managed via Apple In-App Purchases and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the StyleTally experience
  • To deliver daily outfit inspiration and style education cards
  • To process outfit and colour analysis requests
  • To manage your subscription and enforce free tier usage limits
  • To send optional daily reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase: Our backend database and authentication provider. Data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription purchases and entitlements. RevenueCat processes your Apple ID purchase history to determine your subscription status.
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: Used for authentication. We only receive your name and email.
  • Google Gemini: Outfit and colour analysis is powered by Google's Gemini AI. Images you submit are sent to Google Gemini for processing.

3.1. AI-Powered Analysis Disclosure

StyleTally uses Google Gemini, a third-party artificial intelligence service provided by Google LLC, to power outfit and color analysis features. This section explains what data is collected, how it is used, who it is shared with, and the protections in place.

Data Collection and Sharing

What data is collected: When you use the outfit analysis or color analysis feature, StyleTally collects the photo or image you submit through the app.

Who the data is sent to: Your photo is transmitted to Google Gemini (operated by Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA) for AI-powered analysis.

Purpose of data collection: The photo is analyzed by Google Gemini's AI to evaluate your outfit's fit, color harmony, proportions, and styling. The analysis results include a detailed score and personalized fashion recommendations that are returned to you within the app.

User consent: Before your first use of the outfit or color analysis feature, StyleTally will request your explicit permission to share your photo with Google Gemini. You may decline this permission, in which case the analysis feature will not be available. You can modify your preferences at any time within the app settings.

Data Retention and Security

Images are processed in real-time and are not stored or retained by Google Gemini or StyleTally after analysis is complete. Photos are encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS encryption. No facial recognition technology is used — the AI analyzes only clothing items, colors, fit, and styling elements.

Third-Party Data Protection

Google LLC, as the operator of Google Gemini, maintains privacy and security standards that meet or exceed industry requirements. Google's handling of your data is governed by:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Terms of Service
  • Google Gemini API Terms of Service

Google provides the same or equal level of data protection as outlined in this Privacy Policy. StyleTally does not sell, share, or use your photos for any purpose beyond providing your analysis results. Google Gemini does not use submitted images for training or improving its AI models.

Your Control and Rights

You maintain full control over your data:

  • You may choose not to use the analysis feature at any time
  • You can revoke permission for data sharing in the app settings
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app
  • No historical record of analyzed photos is maintained by StyleTally or Google after processing

By using the outfit or color analysis feature and granting permission when prompted, you acknowledge that you have read this disclosure and consent to your photo being transmitted to and processed by Google Gemini in accordance with the terms described above.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

5. Anonymous Users

You may use StyleTally without creating an account. In this case, an anonymous session is created locally on your device. This anonymous session is not linked to any personal information unless you choose to sign in.

6. Children's Privacy

StyleTally is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@style-tally.com.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The StyleTally mobile app does not use cookies. Our website may use minimal analytics to understand visitor traffic. We do not use tracking for advertising purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of StyleTally after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You can request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell your personal information.
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@style-tally.com. We will respond within 45 days of receiving your request.

11. European Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your personal data in certain situations.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

Our legal basis for processing your data includes: consent (for optional features), contract performance (to provide our services), and legitimate interests (to improve our app). To exercise your GDPR rights or lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority, contact us at privacy@style-tally.com.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@style-tally.com.
